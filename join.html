Your club membership dues help to protect and preserve flying sites for all of us! They also support the web site, message board, and the Leonardo XC League.

In 2017 we are moving away from paper membership forms. Please use the links below to enter your personal information electronically. Please feel free to mail your payment, but the treasurer's life is easier if use PayPal.

Fees

  • Annual dues are $50 and are renewed each January
  • Visiting pilots (less than 5 visits) may waive the fee but must complete the membership form and Jefferson County Waiver

To Join:

  1. Tell us who you are. First, use our online membership form.
    RHMPA Online Membership Form
  2. Lookout Pilots - Sign the waiver. Contact the Webmaster  to have the waiver sent to you for an electronic signature.
  3. Pay the dues electronically or send a check payable to the Rocky Mountain Hang Gliding and Paragliding Association, or RMHPA.
