Your club membership dues help to protect and preserve flying sites for all of us! They also support the web site, message board, and the Leonardo XC League.

Fees

  • Annual dues are $40 and are renewed each January
  • Visiting pilots (less than 5 visits) may waive the fee but must complete the membership form and Jefferson County Waiver

To Join:

1) Download the membership form and the Jefferson County Waiver. Fill them both out. You will need Adobe Acrobat Reader to view the forms.

Downloads

  • RMHPA Membership form & Jefferson County Waiver
  • Acrobat Reader

2) Either snail mail the forms to the address below or scan them and email them to the club Treasurer.

3) Pay the dues electronically or send a check payable to the Rocky Mountain Hang Gliding and Paragliding Association, or RMHPA.

Rocky Mountain Hang Gliding and Paragliding Association
