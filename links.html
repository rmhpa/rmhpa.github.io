United States Hang Gliding & Paragliding Association (USHPA)
The United States Hang Gliding & Paragliding Association is proud to serve the great sports of hang gliding and paragliding. Through programs like our Instructor Certification and Pilot Proficiency program, and our award winning magazine, Hang Gliding and Paragliding, the USHPA strives to promote pilot safety, skill, knowledge and above all fun.
