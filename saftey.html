Please use this link to report issues, near misses, and accidents. The survey has been designed to gather information on the types of negative situations RMHPA pilots are encountering such that improvements can be made along with helping other pilots learn from those situations. Your responses will be kept confidential. Thanks for taking the time to complete the survey and many safe flights to you.
