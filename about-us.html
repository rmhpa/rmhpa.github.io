RMHPA provides a member-supported community for hang glider and paraglider pilots and their families, in the Colorado rocky mountain region, further, RMHPA is dedicated to maintaining a local community presence and encourages pilot members to conduct themselves in a safe and respectful manner for the betterment and advocacy of free-flight.

RMHPA Flying Sites

The RMHPA flying sites are on Colorado's Front Range as well as in the mountains. Along the Front Range Lookout (Mt. Zion) and Boulder are the most commonly flown sites. In the mountains RMHPA insures Villa Grove in the San Luis Valley, and the Bellyache and Wollcot sites in the Vail valley. Please see our site guide for more information about our sites.
