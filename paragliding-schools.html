Hang Gliding and Paragliding are governed by the United States Hanggliding and Paragliding Association (USHPA) under an exemption granted by the FAA. Newcommers to the sport need instruction, and a rating granted by USHPA.

The Rmhpa community stronly recomends starting your flying career with a local instructor. Using a local instructor gives you the opportunity to develop a mentor to guide you long after your formal training ends.

Front Range Schools

Vail Valley Paragliding - Vail and Front Range, CO

Peak to Peak Paragliding - Boulder, CO

Parasoft Paragliding and Granger Banks are not certified by USHPA and cannot grant the ratings required to fly paragliders in the United States. Please use a local certified instructor.
